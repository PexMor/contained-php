PHP & Nginx in container

This application was made in good faith that it can help any interested folk to make smart and easy to readu urls that does not contain non-ascii or other weird characters.
It can turn following url: 
http://some.server.com/poll.php/?username=Johnny+English&period=1900-2023&grade=top+secret&reward=>john+girl

into something more legible as: 
http://some.server.com/poll/?uuid=2db9cd01-e909-448d-8883-93e2773657a7

with much less characters that could get corrupted during transfer and as embedded url inside email. It should also be much easier to copy and paste such url. Eventually it can become: 
http://some.server.com/poll/2db9cd01-e909-448d-8883-93e2773657a7

if url rewriting is used
